Lower Level 2.0 is a nostalgic 2000s basement gaming VRChat world featuring an interactive DOS terminal system called Basement OS.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

THE EXPERIENCE

Step into a virtual basement straight from the early 2000s. Complete with wood-paneled walls, vintage gaming setups, and an old CRT monitor running Basement OS - your gateway to achievements, stats, and basement lore.
